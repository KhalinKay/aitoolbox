Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

AIToolBox (kaynai.co.uk) provides free, browser-based AI tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Contact: hello@kaynai.co.uk

Your files — 100% private

Every tool on AIToolBox processes your files entirely within your browser using your own device's CPU and memory. Your photos, PDFs, videos, and other files are never uploaded to any server. We never see, receive, or store them.

What we store locally on your device

We write two items to your browser's localStorage only:

  • aitoolbox_consent — your cookie preference (accept or decline). This never leaves your device.
  • aitoolbox_recent — the last 3 tools you opened, to show a "Welcome back" strip on return visits. This never leaves your device.

Analytics (with consent only)

If you accept cookies via our consent banner, we load Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand aggregate usage patterns — which tools are used most, how long sessions last, and which browsers are common. GA4 data is anonymised and aggregated. We cannot identify individual users. You can opt out at any time using the "Revoke cookie consent" link in the footer. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertising (with consent only)

We display ads via Google AdSense (publisher ID: ca-pub-5242968615107720). If you have consented to cookies, Google may use cookies to show ads relevant to your interests based on your browsing history. You can manage your ad preferences at Google Ad Settings.

Third-party CDNs

JavaScript libraries and AI models are loaded from Cloudflare CDN, jsDelivr, and esm.sh. These services may log technical request data (IP address, browser type, timestamp) per their own privacy policies. They do not receive any of your files or documents.

Your rights (UK GDPR / GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, or erase any personal data we hold. Because all processing is local, there is no server-side account or stored file data to delete. To erase all locally stored data, click "Revoke cookie consent" in the footer, then clear your browser's site data (Settings → Privacy → Clear site data for kaynai.co.uk). For any other data request, contact hello@kaynai.co.uk.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies and similar browser storage technologies store small pieces of information on your device. AIToolBox uses browser localStorage (not HTTP cookies) for essential functions, and Google's cookies for analytics and advertising when you give consent.

Essential storage — no consent required

NamePurposeDuration
aitoolbox_consentStores your cookie preference (accepted or declined)Until you clear browser data
aitoolbox_recentStores the last 3 tools you used for the welcome-back stripUntil you clear browser data

These items are stored on your device only and are never transmitted to any server.

Analytics cookies — consent required

If you accept our consent banner, we load Google Analytics 4, which sets _ga and _ga_* cookies. These measure aggregate usage such as page views and session duration. All data is anonymised. Duration: up to 13 months.

Advertising cookies — consent required

If you accept, Google AdSense may set cookies to personalise ads shown on this site based on your interests. Duration: up to 13 months. See Google's advertising policy.

Managing consent

Click "Revoke cookie consent" in the site footer at any time to withdraw consent. Analytics and advertising scripts will not load until you grant consent again. You can also clear all site data via your browser settings.

Browser / device opt-out

Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to block GA4 entirely, or use your browser's built-in privacy settings to block third-party cookies.
